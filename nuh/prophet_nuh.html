Prophet Noah
The Great Ark & Allah's Love 🚢
"And We carried him on a vessel made of planks and nails." — Quran 54:13

🌙 When People Forgot Allah
Long ago, after Prophet Adam, people slowly forgot who made them.

They carved statues from wood and stone and began to worship them instead of Allah — believing the statues could hear them and protect them.

But a statue is only wood and stone. It cannot hear. It cannot love. Only Allah can do all of that.
Ibn ʿArabī teaches: Allah is too big for any box or shape. لَيْسَ كَمِثْلِهِ شَيْءٌ — "There is nothing like Him." (Quran 42:11)
📢 950 Years of Calling
Allah sent Noah to remind his people of the truth.

Noah called them every single day. In the morning. At night. Quietly. Loudly. "Come back to Allah," he said. "He made you. He loves you. These statues cannot hear you."

But the people covered their ears and walked away. For 950 years.
Ibn ʿArabī says: Noah carried Allah's love to a people who did not want it yet. True love never gives up — it calls, and calls, and calls.
🔨 A Boat in the Desert?!
After 950 years, the people still refused. So Allah told Noah: "No one else will believe. Do not grieve over what they used to do." (Quran 11:36)

Allah decided to relieve Noah from them — and gave him a new mission: "Build a great ark." Allah said: "Noah, build a great ark under Our eyes and as We reveal to you." (Quran 11:37)

There was no sea nearby. Just dry, cracked earth. So Noah built his ark right there — in the middle of the desert. The people laughed: "Where is your sea, Noah?" Noah just smiled and kept building.
Ibn ʿArabī teaches: tawakkul — true trust in Allah — means doing your best and leaving the rest to Him, even when others think you are crazy. That is the highest kind of faith.

🐘 Loading the Ark!
When Allah's command came, He told Noah: "Take into the ark a pair of every kind — male and female — and your family and those who believe." (Quran 11:40)

And so Noah loaded pairs of the animals he needed onto the ark, two by two.

But wait — a flood? Yes. That is exactly why Allah asked Noah to build the ark. A great flood was coming. And the ark would keep them all safe.

Every kind that was needed — carefully, faithfully, as Allah had commanded.
Ibn ʿArabī says: Every creature in the world knows Allah and praises Him in its own way. The whole universe is full of Allah's presence.
🌧️ The Rain Begins!
Then the sky turned dark. Drip. Drop. SPLASH. Rain started to fall — more and more and more!

The water rose higher and higher. But Noah and all the animals on the ark were safe and warm inside.

Noah held his hands up high: "Alhamdulillah, Ya Allah. You protect us!"
Ibn ʿArabī says: The flood was not only a punishment — it was Allah washing the world clean. Even in difficulty, there is mercy hidden inside.
💔 The Son Who Refused
As the waters rose, Noah's heart ached. His own son had not believed.

Noah called to him one last time: "My son! Come with us! Do not be with those who deny!"

But his son said: "I will climb a mountain — it will protect me."

A great wave rose between them. No mountain can save us from Allah's decree. Only faith carries us through.
Ibn ʿArabī teaches: even the greatest prophets feel grief. Noah's pain for his son was real — and Allah answered him with wisdom and gentleness. Love and loss are both from Allah.

🌙 Safe on the Waves
For many days and nights, the ark floated on the great water.

Inside, the animals were cozy. Everyone was at peace.

Every night, Noah would look at the stars and say: "Subhanallah. Allah made all of this — and He is taking care of all of us."
Ibn ʿArabī teaches: Peace — sakīna — is a gift from Allah. It lives in the heart that trusts. Noah's heart was full of it, even on stormy waters.
🌅 "O Earth — Swallow Your Water!"
Then Allah commanded: "O earth — swallow your water. O sky — stop." (Quran 11:44)

The water fell. Slowly. Gently. Mountain tops appeared. Then land.

A little white dove flew back with a green olive branch in her beak. The earth was alive again.
Ibn ʿArabī says: when Allah commands, all creation obeys with love — the earth, the sky, the water. The whole universe is in constant conversation with Allah.
🌈 Allah's Promise — the Rainbow!
The water went down, and the ark rested on dry land. The animals ran out and the world was green and beautiful again!

Then Allah painted the sky with a rainbow.

It was Allah's way of saying: "I love you. I will always protect those who trust Me." 🌈
Ibn ʿArabī says: The rainbow has seven colors because Allah's beauty has no limit — it keeps going and going, like love that never ends.

Your Flood. Your Ark.

Noah's flood was water. Your flood is different — but it is still a flood.

Maybe your flood is when friends laugh at you for doing what is right. Or when you try your best and still fail. Or when you feel alone.

That heavy, hard feeling? That is your flood.

Noah's ark was made of wood. Your ark is made of something stronger.

🕌 Salah 📖 Quran 🤲 Du'a ❤️ Family

Every time you choose Allah in a hard moment — you are adding a plank to your ark.

💭 Think of one moment today that was hard. That was your flood.
Now think of one thing that kept you safe. That was your ark.
